OTC Talks Rules

  • The mode of OTC Talks is Online.
  • Duration of the lightning talk: 15 - 25 minutes from 10:45 PM IST on a Saturday, followed by the CatchUp on the respective day.
  • QnA/Discussion after the talk: About 15 minutes.
  • The Talks are open for all and can be attended by anyone.
  • IMPORTANT:
    • Only topics from Tech and related fields (Eg: mental health, salary negotiations, etc) are permitted.
    • We highly encourage demonstrations over presentations, but speakers are free to do as they please.
    • Promotional or sponsored content is not permitted.
  • Please follow the Code of Conduct while attending our talks. Any violations can be reported to the organisers or on contact@ourtech.community.
