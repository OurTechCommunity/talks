The Browser Backstage - Peek in Perf

Speaker: Vivek Galatage

Abstract

Users often complain that their browsers are slow, use too many resources, or are memory hogs. This talk explores how browser engineers diagnose and address these issues, focusing on performance measurement tools in Chromium-based browsers. We’ll also examine major security exploits like Spectre and Meltdown in the context of modern CPU architectures.
