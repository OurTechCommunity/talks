Internals of Email Relay

Speaker: Omkar Khair

Abstract

Join to learn more how Omkar built a email relay for himself using AWS and Firebase. He will share his learning of the SMTP protocol and other services involved that allowed him to run the project for 3 years without maintainence. He will also share what broke after 3 years and what he plans to do to mitigate this.

Know more about the underlying concepts about email.
